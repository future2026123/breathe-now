Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Panic Breathing - Anxiety Relief ("Breathe Now", "the App") is developed by SystemLab Co. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Our app is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Health or medical data

Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (such as sound on/off settings and language selection) locally on your device using standard device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any users, we are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
